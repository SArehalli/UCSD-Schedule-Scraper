197 Academic Internship Program
To apply, students must have completed 90 units and have a minimum of 2.5 UC GPA (unless higher GPA is required for a specific field or industry). Applicants attend an orientation and submit an application via InternLink at http://aipinternlink.ucsd.edu The AIP office is located in the Literature Building, room 210. Walk-in hours are Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday 8am to 12pm and 1 - 4pm and on Wednesday 8am to 1pm. Students must apply one quarter before they want to intern. The Academic Internship Program begins accepting applications the 2nd week of each quarter.
